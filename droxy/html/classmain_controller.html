Detailed Description

mainController's function is to put together the inputs from GUI and Monttaeller and then send continuously updates to GUI about how much money is missing to pay It inherits from OSAPI ThreadFunctor to make it able to communicate with GUI and MontTaeller After the customer has reached the amount it has to pay for the rope mainController sends to snorHaandtering and montDosering by the i2c class how much rope the customer is gonna get and how much change it gets
